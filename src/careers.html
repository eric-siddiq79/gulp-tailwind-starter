Careers

Come transform the data center industry with us. If you’re collaborative, curious, and eager to make an impact, we think you will like it here.

Do great work from anywhere

At Rowan, we know that great work can be done from anywhere. We offer a remote-friendly work environment supported by the teamwork, technology, and in-office gathering time to help you be successful. We are headquartered in Denver, Colorado and have offices in San Francisco, California, Seattle, Washington, and Traverse City, Michigan. Learn from the best

Our team consists of experienced leaders who have worked with key hyperscale companies, real estate developers and large-scale engineering and construction firms. We’re eager to share our journey and get to know yours.

We Are Here To Support You

We want to make sure you can do great work. We offer competitive benefits focused on helping you be and achieve your best.

  • A hybrid working environment, with opportunities for remote work
  • Team building and educational opportunities
  • Generous benefits package including employer paid health insurance, unlimited PTO, 401(k) and a competitive compensation and incentive plan
  • Office setup benefit for remote team members
  • Wellness and technology stipends
