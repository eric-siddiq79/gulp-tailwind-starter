Bauxite, located within the 2,000-acre development near Frederick, Maryland, is planned as an initial multi-building 300 MW campus. Furthermore, the campus plan incorporates expansion, which will lead to a doubling of the building size and an increase in power capacity to over 450MW. It boasts near-term power interconnection dates, competitive power pricing, a range of fiber options, and water and wastewater infrastructure. With advantageous General/Light Industrial zoning in place, Bauxite is poised to deliver the initial 300 MW by July 2025, providing a high value alternative to the congested Ashburn corridor.

Our meticulous approach involves the careful selection and development of data infrastructure in locations that are not just optimal, but strategic. These handpicked sites are chosen for their inherent advantages in connectivity, accessibility, and scalability. But that’s just the beginning. Beyond these prime locations, we go the extra mile by crafting custom-powered shells that align seamlessly with your operational needs. These shells are purpose-built to support your data center’s unique requirements, ensuring a foundation of reliability and efficiency.


Rowan’s projects extend beyond data centers, aiming to enhance communities. Sustainability is at our core, with each project designed to minimize ecological impact. We integrate renewables, prioritize energy efficiency, and champion a greener future. Our commitment to creating value goes beyond technology, positively impacting communities and advancing sustainability.
