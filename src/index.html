We rapidly deliver customized data center campuses with a focus on sustainability.

Whether it’s powered shells or fully built data center campuses, our approach is to invest responsibly in sustainable data center infrastructure and to quickly develop customized build-to-suit data center infrastructure.

OUR VISION

Development Expedited

We accelerate data center delivery by developing a portfolio of entitled, interconnected and build-ready projects, saving our hyperscale partners years in development time. In as little as 12-18 months, we deliver the first phase of a data center campus built to our partner’s specifications and to the highest standard for quality and sustainability.

OUR PARTNER FOCUSED APPROACH

Empowering Your Vision

We seamlessly integrate with our hyperscale partners, becoming an extension of their teams. We deliver fully outsourced data center campus environments based on our partner’s design requirements. Our expert end-to-end team encompasses land experts, developers, energy procurement leaders, designers, and builders, all in one.

GOING BEYOND PROBLEM-SOLVING

Delivery the Rowan Way

Our focus is efficiency, quality, and flexibility. We are fueled by innovation and help our partners solve any challenge. Our solutions are customized to our partners’ needs. We build their design and collaborate to execute on their campus vision, while creating a space for future growth.

RESPONSIBLE IMPACT

Partners In Sustainability

We are deeply committed to driving positive change through sustainable data center development. We advance our hyperscale partners’ net zero goals through 100% renewable energy access, responsible resource stewardship, and sustainable materials and construction practices. Our dedication to responsible practices is at the core of our mission, and we take immense pride in our ability to seamlessly align with and fulfill our clients’ sustainability requirements. As a “Dark Green” Article 9 Fund (European sustainability standard), our sustainability efforts are ingrained in every facet of our delivery and operations, underpinned by thoughtfully defined sustainable investment objectives.

FEATURED DEVELOPMENT

Opening Summer 2025
300 MW Hyperscale Campus

The Frederick Data Center project is a 151-acre campus currently in development and ready for operation in early 2025. The Frederick Data Center sits within Quantum Loophole’s master planned Frederick Clean Cloud Community and has the potential for expansion up to 300 MW within 1-2 years. It offers a total capacity of 450 MW at semi-adjacent sites within the community.
