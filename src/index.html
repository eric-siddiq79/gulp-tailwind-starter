RESPONSIBLE IMPACT

Partners In Sustainability

We are deeply committed to driving positive change through sustainable data center development. We advance our hyperscale partners’ net zero goals through 100% renewable energy access, responsible resource stewardship, and sustainable materials and construction practices. Our dedication to responsible practices is at the core of our mission, and we take immense pride in our ability to seamlessly align with and fulfill our clients’ sustainability requirements. As a “Dark Green” Article 9 Fund (European sustainability standard), our sustainability efforts are ingrained in every facet of our delivery and operations, underpinned by thoughtfully defined sustainable investment objectives.

FEATURED DEVELOPMENT

Opening Summer 2025
300 MW Hyperscale Campus

The Frederick Data Center project is a 151-acre campus currently in development and ready for operation in early 2025. The Frederick Data Center sits within Quantum Loophole’s master planned Frederick Clean Cloud Community and has the potential for expansion up to 300 MW within 1-2 years. It offers a total capacity of 450 MW at semi-adjacent sites within the community.
