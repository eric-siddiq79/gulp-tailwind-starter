Our Team Brings Together Top Names in The Industry

We deliver customized data center solutions as fast or faster than our hyperscale partners can do it alone.

Rowan Digital Infrastructure, LLC was established in November 2020 to support hyperscale data center users in meeting their development needs sustainably. With our flexible build-to-suit offering, we can deliver customized and configurable solutions that provide hyperscalers the flexibility they need to scale fast.


Our build-to-suit offering allows us to customize our solutions and provide our partners the flexibility to scale fast. We are actively developing multiple, strategically located sites across the U.S. to host next-generation, mission critical, hyperscale data centers.

Deep Knowledge & Expertise

Be confident knowing you are working with the best talent the industry has to offer. We offer decades of developer, builder and hyperscale data center delivery experience. We have assembled an industry-leading team that spans the data center project life cycle, including:

  • Site origination
  • Land acquisition & development
  • Economic Development & Government
  • Energy infrastructure & procurement
  • Design & Engineering
  • Construction
  • Project finance
  • Facility operations
  • Asset & strategic portfolio management
